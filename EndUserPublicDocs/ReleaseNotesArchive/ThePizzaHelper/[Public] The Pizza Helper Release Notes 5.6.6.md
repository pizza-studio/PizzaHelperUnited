// Major changes introduced in The Pizza Helper v5.6.6:

- New behavior: On loading the Character Inventory View, the app will now reset the local EnkaDB cache if necessary. It's just that the data change is less likely to be reflected to the UI until the next time you load the Character Inventory View.
- Fixed some rare cases of concurrency inefficiency problems.
- Fixed an issue hindering certain UI assets of embedded widgets from being loaded on early releases of OS25.

In the worst-case scenario, the app might be removed from the App Store and get re-released under a different team member's name and a new App Bundle ID: This app supports macOS and uses Group Containers, making it unable to meet App Store Connect’s criteria for a developer transfer. This means that if such re-release happens, it won't be able to access data from the original app. You will only be able to use your existing data backups to restore your information in the new app. We strongly recommend that you regularly back up your gacha records and local account data. A final decision may be made by the end of March 2026. Please also note that the new app may be temporarily unavailable for iOS users in mainland China due to the need to re-apply for an ICP record.)
